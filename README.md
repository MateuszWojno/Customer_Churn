# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **customer churn analysis** built in Excel, combining data processing, KPI design, and business insights in a clean dashboard format.

The goal was to identify **key drivers of churn** and propose **actionable recommendations** based on the analysis.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer churn behavior
* Identify high-risk segments
* Understand factors influencing churn
* Provide business-driven recommendations

---

## 📌 Key KPIs

* **Total Customers:** 10,000
* **Churn Rate:** 25.28% 🔴
* **Retention Rate:** 74.72% 🟢
* **High-Risk Customers:** 144 🟠
* **High-Risk %:** 1.44%

## 📈 Dashboard Insights


### 🔴 Customer Behavior

* Churn is significantly higher among **inactive customers**
* Customers with **only one product** have the highest churn rate (42%)
* Churn decreases as the number of products increases

---

### 🌍 Geography

* **Germany** shows the highest churn rate among analyzed countries
* Indicates potential regional issues requiring deeper investigation

---

### 📊 Segmentation

* Majority of customers fall into **low-risk segment (~70%)**
* A small but critical portion represents **high-risk customers (~1.4%)**

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

> Customers with low engagement and limited product usage are the most likely to churn.

---

## 🛠️ Recommendations

* 🎯 **Focus retention efforts on inactive customers**
* 📦 **Increase product adoption (cross-sell strategies)**
* 🌍 **Investigate churn drivers in the German market**
* 🚀 **Improve onboarding and early customer engagement**
